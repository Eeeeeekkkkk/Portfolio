Best Services We Provide For Our Clients

Free Returns

We take great satisfaction in providing outstanding customer service at every turn. Because we always want you to be happy, we provide free returns on every item. Please get in touch with our helpful customer service staff if for any reason you're not happy with your purchase, and we'll walk you through the simple return procedure. Your satisfaction is our first concern, and we work hard to make sure you have a flawless and joyful buying experience with us.

Custom Orders

Whether you have a specific design in mind, require custom sizing, or desire a particular length, our skilled artisans are here to bring your vision to life. Please be aware that the price and turnaround time for bespoke orders might change based on several variables, including the length that is wanted, size requirements, and design intricacy. In close collaboration with you, our staff will present you with a customized price and project the length of time it will take to produce your order. Custom orders normally need around two weeks to complete from the time of order placing to delivery. But please be aware that large orders or sophisticated designs could take longer.

Shipping

To make sure that you enjoy our high-quality nail products wherever you are, we provide international delivery. Depending on your region, we have different delivery costs, however, we are happy to provide free shipping on purchases above $80. We'll make sure your order arrives at you quickly and safely, no matter how far away you live. Shipping costs for goods under $80 will be determined at checkout using your destination address. You may be confident that we collaborate with reputable shipping partners to offer dependable support and tracking details at your convenience.
